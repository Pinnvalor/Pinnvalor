    8. Real-Time Alerts & Notifications

    Modern MIS systems can be configured to send alerts when performance deviates from predefined thresholds. Examples include:

    • Revenue Drop Alert: Notify management if sales drop below a set percentage.

    • Compliance Alerts: Warn stakeholders about pending regulatory deadlines or breaches.
