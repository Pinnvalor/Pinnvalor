What Are AIFs?

At their core, Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) are private investment vehicles that pool money from accredited investors to invest in non-traditional assets like private equity, real estate, commodities, and venture capital.

AIFs allow you to access investments that are typically out of reach for individual investors, providing exclusive opportunities that may not be available on the stock market.
